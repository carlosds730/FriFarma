About us

Frifarma SRL was founded on July 2005 with the aim of beginning the importation and distribution of generic drugs of top level brands with exclusivity for the Dominican Republic. The first supplier was Richet’s Labs, an Argentinean lab with 50 years of experience. In 2006 we obtained the license from the Dominican Health authorities and since that moment began the work of sanitary registration of our main products.

Paying attention to the interest of the Dominican Health authorities in the reduction of (mortalidad infantil) our sells began with the (Surfactatne Pulmonar Bovino) to the entity Promese. Then we started to introduce our main products (mostly (anti-infectious)) in some of the most important health centers of the country like Plaza de la Salud-Cedimat-HOMS among others.

Between the 2008 and 2010 were registered products from other important labs: KUP from Corea and BDR from India. In 2014 Cuban’s CIMAB grant us the commercial representation.

From our first years until the present we have been growing up, acquiring new clients every day with hard work and the quality of our products.
